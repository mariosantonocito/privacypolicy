Privacy Policy

Location Data — SAROMS Mobile Application

What data is collected

The SAROMS Mobile application collects precise GPS location data, including when the application is running in the background or the device screen is off.

Why it is collected

Background location is used exclusively to support real-time coordination and personnel safety during Search and Rescue (SAR) operations. It allows the organisation operating the app to track team members in the field and coordinate emergency response activities.

How it is used and shared

Location data is transmitted in real time solely to the server operated and controlled by the customer organisation that deployed the SAROMS Mobile application (e.g. a Search and Rescue authority, civil protection agency, or equivalent body). This organisation acts as the independent Data Controller for all data collected through the app, pursuant to EU Regulation 679/2016 (GDPR).

Location data is never sent to, accessed by, or stored by Leonardo S.p.A. Leonardo S.p.A. acts solely as the technology licensor and does not process location data at any stage.

User control

You can revoke background location permission at any time in your device settings:
Settings → Apps → SAROMS → Permissions → Location
Revoking this permission will prevent the app from transmitting your position to the coordinating organisation, which may affect operational safety features.

For any questions about how your location data is processed, stored, or retained, please contact the organisation that provided you access to the SAROMS Mobile application, as they are the sole Data Controller responsible for those activities.
